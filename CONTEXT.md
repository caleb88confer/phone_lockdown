# Phone Lockdown 

## What we are building
A mobile app that helps users manage digital distractions by blocking apps and websites. The app uses a physical key in the way of scanning a bar code or QR code in order to add enough friction to the process to help users form more healthy habits surrounding distracting apps. The app also implements some game style unlockables to further insentivize app usage. The app is free to download and use. Their will either be a couple pay to unlock items, or a donation model.
